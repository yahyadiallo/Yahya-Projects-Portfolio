# Washington EV Registration Analysis

## Overview

This project explores electric vehicle adoption patterns across Washington State using public EV registration data. The goal was to understand which cities have the highest concentrations of electric vehicles and identify the vehicle manufacturers that dominate those markets.

The visualization uses a D3.js heatmap to compare EV registration counts across Washington's top cities and most common vehicle brands. The project highlights how EV adoption is concentrated in larger urban areas and reveals differences in manufacturer popularity across locations.

---

## Research Question

**How are electric vehicle registrations distributed across Washington cities, and which manufacturers dominate the market?**

As electric vehicle adoption continues to grow, understanding where EVs are most common can provide insight into regional adoption trends and consumer preferences. This project investigates EV registration patterns using publicly available state data.

## Dataset

### Electric Vehicle Population Data

Washington State Department of Licensing

The dataset contains electric vehicle registration records across Washington State, including vehicle manufacturers and registration locations.

Dataset Source:

https://data.wa.gov/Transportation/Electric-Vehicle-Population-Data/f6w7-q2d2/about_data

---

## Methodology

1. Imported electric vehicle registration data from a CSV file.
2. Cleaned records with missing city or manufacturer information.
3. Identified the top eight cities with the highest EV registration counts.
4. Identified the top eight vehicle manufacturers by registration amount.
5. Aggregated registration counts for each city-manufacturer combination.
6. Built a D3.js heatmap to visualize registration patterns across cities and manufacturers.
7. Applied a color scale to highlight areas with higher registration concentrations.

---

## Key Findings

- EV registrations are concentrated in larger Washington cities, particularly Seattle.
- Seattle recorded substantially more EV registrations than any other city included in the analysis.
- Tesla was the most common manufacturer across all top cities.
- Other manufacturers such as Chevrolet, Nissan, Kia, Toyota, BMW, Ford, and Hyundai showed significantly lower registration counts.
- The heatmap demonstrates how EV adoption patterns vary geographically while highlighting Tesla's strong market presence.
